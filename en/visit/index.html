Essential Information for First-Time Visitors

There’s ample parking in the Doha Oasis Mall complex, where Doha Quest is located. Basement parking is free for Doha Quest visitors - just make sure you get your parking ticket stamped at our information desk. Present your stamped parking ticket when exiting the parking lot. We also offer valet parking at the main entrance at 30 QAR for standard services and 60 QAR for VIP services.

There is an ATM machine available near Quest's main entrance.

We have a number of lockers available for guests to rent out. Kindly visit our Information desk near Quest's main entrance in order to check availability.

Mothers can tend to their babies' needs in our baby changing & baby feeding room, located in the Clubhouse, in Oryxville.

Guests can rent strollers for their little ones, available at Quest's information desk near the main entrance.

There are prayer rooms available near Space Cantina, in Gravity.

Smoking within Quest is strictly prohibited. For the comfort of all guests, Quest is smoke-free. The smoking of tobacco, e-cigarettes, or other products that produce vapor or smoke is allowed only in designated outdoor smoking areas.

Guests can rent wheelchairs by enquiring at our information desk, near the entrance counters.

Our guest services colleagues will assist you by checking our lost & found items. Give us a call any time at 44103444 or directly inquire at our information desk, near the entrance counters.

Doha Quest is easily accessible by bus and metro. If you plan on taking the bus, the M108 route is the best way to reach us. Metro users can either get off at Al Bidda or Msheirab station - they are both within walking distance from Doha Quest.

For safety reasons, we do not allow photography on board rides. If you would like your picture taken while on any of our rides, our staff can help you. At other times, you are free to document your day at Doha Quest on your phone or personal cameras. Prior permission from the concerned authorities is required for professional equipment and shoots. Contact us for more information.

If you have belongings that need safekeeping while you’re exploring Doha Quest, you can make use of our locker facilities. Visit the Guest Services Information desk for directions once you arrive.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where exactly is Doha Quest located?

Doha Quest is located at Gate 2, Doha Oasis Mall on Al Khaleej Street, Msheireb, Doha, Qatar. You can easily locate us with GPS. We are also easily accessible by bus and metro.

  • If you’re taking the bus: Book the M108 route.
  • If you’re riding the metro, you have two options that are both within walking distance from Doha Quest:
    • Al Bidda station
    • Msheireb station
What are the visiting hours of Doha Quest?
We are open Monday through Saturday, from 2:00 PM to 10:00 PM, and stay closed on Sunday for most of the year. However, our opening hours may vary during Ramadan. Check our official website for updated timings before visiting us.
How can I get to Doha Quest via public transportation?
Doha Quest is easily accessible via the metro. You can take a metro to Al Bidda or Msheireb metro station. Our indoor theme park is just a short walk away. You can also take the M108 bus route.
Is there adequate parking or car access?
Yes, easy car access and ample parking are available at the Doha Oasis Mall complex, where Doha Quest is located. If this is your first visit, we recommend using GPS to locate us easily.
Which mall is Doha Quest inside?
Doha Quest is located inside the Doha Oasis Mall in the Msheireb district. It is Qatar’s first indoor theme park and has rides and attractions for all ages.
