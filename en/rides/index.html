Quest is an indoor theme park in Qatar comprised of more than 30 rides & attractions, featuring a mix of thrill-based, family-oriented, and kid-focused experiences tailored for an audience of all ages. At Quest, customers get to Explore the Past. Play in the Present. Imagine the Future. At Quest, There’s Always More…

MAGMA BLAST

The World’s Tallest Indoor Drop Tower Experience the world’s tallest indoor drop tower with the Magma Blast at Doha Quest; a crowd-puller and a favourite among thrill-seekers. This media-enhanced shot-and-drop tower simulates a massive research probe as it taps into the potent geothermal energies beneath the Earth before blasting you high into the sky.
  • Minimum height for unaccompanied persons 140 cm
  • Total Capacity 12 PAX
  • Minimum height for accompanied persons 130 cm
  • Maximum height 195 cm
  • Duration 3 minutes

Epiq Coaster

The World’s Tallest Indoor Roller Coaster Experience the world’s tallest indoor roller coaster at Doha Quest! Strap in for the ride of your life on EpiQ Coaster- the revolutionary solar-powered vessel endorsed by Spike the space-hedgehog. Experience thrilling speeds of 91 km/hr as you hurtle around Gravity Station. With exhilarating drops, twists and turns, this roller coaster in Qatar is not for the faint-hearted.
  • Total Capacity 12 pax
  • Minimum height 140 cm
  • Maximum height 195 CM
  • ride Duration 90 seconds

iFly Quest

Indoor Skydiving iFly Quest is an experience that will take guests on an exciting flying adventure. Guests can enjoy the thrill and adrenaline of a free fall experience as they float on a smooth cushion of air.
  • MINIMUM HEIGHT 100 CM
  • Minimum age 3 years old
  • Maximum Weight 118 kg

Quest for Speed & Oryx Speedway

Racing Simulators Quest for Speed and Oryx Speedway are racing simulators that will take guests on an exciting adventure of driving a racing car. This attraction will exhibit movements that include motion simulation, twists, and sudden turns. This Quest attraction requires participants to use screens mounted on a moving platform, interactive gear shifts, accelerator and brake pedals, and a steering wheel.

Gravity Swing

Pendulum swing This spinning swing ride will make young adrenaline junkies giddy with excitement. It has outward-facing seats suspended from a large, artificial gravity generator, which will have passengers wildly spinning and swinging as the ground gets further and further away. Buckle up and get ready to face intense solar storms and help the Dobots maintain control of Gravity Station in this fun thematic ride.
  • Total Capacity 16 pax
  • Minimum height for unaccompanied persons 130 cm
  • Minimum height for accompanied persons 120 cm
  • ride Duration 90 seconds

Aero Flip

A High-Speed Centrifugal Ride This Doha Quest ride takes guests on an amazing journey that recreates the experiences and sensations of an acrobatic aeroplane flight. Get your heart racing with banks, loops, and dives, and enjoy the thrilling feeling of weightlessness. You can also expect spins and rotations, so be prepared.
  • Total Capacity 12 PAX
  • Minimum height for accompanied persons 132 cm
  • Minimum height for unaccompanied persons 140 cm

Time Tumbler

Unicoaster Tumble and spin on a spectacular clockwork mechanism, marking the crossroads of past, present, and future. This thrilling centrepiece of the City of Imagination features rider-controlled inversions, allowing you to set the intensity to your preference as you roll along an undulating track.
  • Total Capacity 16 pax
  • Minimum height 120 cm
  • ride Duration 3 minutes 25 seconds

Robomania

A dynamic industrial robot arm This ride exhibits movements that include heaving, pitching and rolling, with a unique blend of large sweeping motions. Different intensities can be selected for a customised experience.

LASER OASIS

Team recreational activity This competitive team sport involves recreational shooting. Players use infrared-emitting light guns to tag designated targets and score points while avoiding being hit by competitors. Participants must wear closed shoes. Children under the age of six are not allowed.

Time Travel

Feel the thrill A family thrill ride in which four arms rotate in fast intermeshing orbits, while the seats remain upright, and the central tower rotates around itself. The hypnotic movement is like a visual riddle that’s as much fun to watch as it is to ride.
  • Total Capacity 32 pax
  • Minimum height for unaccompanied persons 130 cm
  • Minimum height for accompanied persons 120 cm
  • Ride Duration 90 seconds

Gravity Wheel

A High-Speed Centrifugal Ride Doha Quest’s Gravity Wheel takes guests on a spinning adventure. Shaped like a cylinder with translucent walls, the wheel allows those waiting in line to see passengers who have gone before them spinning at alarming speeds, charging the atmosphere with anticipation.
  • Total Capacity 24 pax
  • Minimum height 130 cm
  • Duration 2 minutes

Oryx Express

Family Roller Coaster Hop aboard a caravan of off-road vehicles and race around the rocky lands of Oryxville on a pleasantly thrilling roller coaster adventure for the whole family.
  • Total Capacity 16 pax
  • Minimum height for unaccompanied persons 130 cm
  • Minimum height for accompanied persons 105 cm
  • Maximum height 195 cm
  • Ride Duration 90 seconds

VR Fun Station

Outlandish Adventures In this VR attraction for two, guests wear VR gear and get to experience several exciting virtual adventures. The simulator creates a vivid experience with added effects like movements and vibrations.
  • Total Capacity 2 pax
  • Minimum height 135 cm
  • Duration 5 minutes

Spike’s Astro Tower

Media-enhanced tilt-drop tower Join Spike, space hedgehog, for a look at Gravity Station’s fuel-harvesting process by ascending one of the station’s Qalbium Collection Towers. At the top, we watch adorable Dobots as they mine Qalbium from incoming asteroids. When a massive asteroid smashes into our viewport, we go plummeting back down!
  • Total Capacity 12 pax
  • Minimum height for unaccompanied persons 130 cm
  • Minimum height for accompanied persons 105 cm
  • Maximum height 200 cm
  • Duration 90 seconds

FLY WITH FLAP

A Zipline Ride This Quest ride is the final part of an ultimate climbing challenge that requires players to ascend a towering spire that pierces Quest’s ceiling. You are then securely seated in an individual zipline car, suspended from an overhead coaster, and get to enjoy a bird’s eye view of the whole park.
  • Total Capacity 1 PAX
  • MINIMUM HEIGHT 120 CM
  • MAXIMUM HEIGHT 180 CM
  • DURATION 30 SECONDS

  • MAXIMUM WEIGHT 113 KILOGRAMS

GLIDE WITH FIRNAS

  • DURATION 5 MINUTES

Bumper Cars Race around with your friends in our classic bumper cars! A wonder zone where friends bump into each other guarded by Magis The Magnificent
  • Minimum height for accompanied persons 105 cm

  • Maximum Height 195 cm

Carousel

A Timeless Family-Friendly Ride No theme park experience is complete without a Carousel. This classic attraction offers timeless fun for the entire family to enjoy!
  • Total Capacity 48 pax

  • Minimum height for accompanied persons 105 cm

Wings of Destiny

A journey through time and space A 3D Flying Theater ride during which eccentric inventor Firnas and his feathered friend Flap go on a thrilling, high-flying, time-hopping 3D animated adventure that sends us soaring aboard Firnas’ newest creation, the Flying Time Machine! The experience is enhanced by offboard special effects such as wind, scents and water spritzes.
  • Total Capacity 39 pax
  • Minimum height for unaccompanied persons 140 cm
  • Minimum height for accompanied persons 105 cm
  • Maximum height 190 cm

Legend of the Golden Oryx

3D Motion Simulator A theatre-seating, 3D motion simulator, and enchanting adventure, that takes us on a journey to a fantasy realm inhabited by magical oryxes and the mysterious Golden Oryx.
  • Total Capacity 30 pax
  • Minimum height for unaccompanied persons 130 cm
  • Minimum height for accompanied persons 105 cm

Dune Bashers

Children’s bumper cars This whimsical bumper car ride allows children and their guardians to smash and bash as they drive miniature Sports Utility Vehicles around the desert ruins of Oryxville.
  • Total Capacity 20 pax with maximum 10 adults at a time
  • Minimum height for unaccompanied persons 130 cm
  • Minimum height for accompanied persons 105 cm
  • Maximum Height 195 cm
  • Ride Duration 90 seconds

Roll-A-Ball

Family fun 6-player Roll-a-ball is a fun game for all ages. Players roll their ball into one of three speed zones, activating their target to move toward the finish line. The one who finishes first wins the game!

Balloon Bust

What's poppin'? Players are to use speed and accuracy to throw a weighted bag at a wall of balloons. Whoever pops the most is crowned the winner!

Hot shot basketball

Shoot the hoop Short Range Basketball is a typical shoot-the-hoop game where guests need to try to get the ball through the basket from a short distance. Whoever shoots the most hoops wins the game!

Bazooka Blast

Knock 'em down! This game uses low-impact foam balls to knock down its targets. Players go head-to-head to see who can knock down their targets the fastest!

PLAYGROUND

Kids’ play area The Playground is a 2-level kids’ playground in the shape of a giant box. Kids can enjoy sliding down tunnel tubes and spiral slides, bouncing on giant yoga balls, swinging on hanging props and climbing on fun structures. Our playground will keep children busy having fun for hours.
  • MINIMUM AGE 3 YEARS OLD
  • MAXIMUM AGE 12 YEARS OLD

Frog Jump

A Bouncy Frog-Themed Ride Ride giant frogs and experience what it’s like to jump like one! This bouncy ride is a hit among children and fun to enjoy as a family.

Pirate Ship

A Pirate Ship-Themed Swing Swing high above the seven seas in this miniature pirate ship-themed ride. An all-time classic, this ride will rock you back and forth in a pendulum motion, with increasing height and momentum for a thrilling experience.

Each of the six cars can accommodate four children, or one adult and two children. The Ferris Wheel is a theme park classic for a reason, and this mini version is a hit among children and young families.

This whimsical, interactive ride always brings big smiles on children’s faces as they pilot their way up and down.

With fast and unexpected turns, and soft ups and downs, it captures the spirit of a roller coaster while still being a family-friendly experience. It’s the ideal place to start for roller coaster newbies or those looking to build their appetite for adventure.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the most popular rides at Doha Quest?

Top Doha Quest rides include the EpiQ Coaster - the world’s tallest indoor roller coaster, and the Magma Blast - the world’s tallest drop tower. The Gravity Swing and the Time Tumbler are also popular among those who enjoy high-adrenaline experiences. 

Are there VR or simulator rides at Doha Quest?

Yes! Doha Quest features cutting-edge virtual reality rides and simulators that combine immersive storytelling with state-of-the-art motion technology. 

Does Doha Quest have water rides or indoor splash zones?

No, Doha Quest does not currently feature any water rides. But there are plenty of indoor attractions suitable for all ages, including multi-player Doha Quest games that you can enjoy with family and friends. 

Can I ride everything in one visit to Doha Quest? 

Yes, you can experience all the major Doha Quest rides in a single visit! Just add Time Portal (Fast Track*) to cut down wait times. Time Portal gives priority access on select rides, helping you do more in less time. Book online to skip the ticket counter and dive straight into the adventure!

*Fast Track is available on select rides.
