About the Camp

Give your child a summer filled with adventure, learning, creativity, and excitement at Quest Summer Camp 2026.

Designed for children aged 6 to 12 years, our summer camp combines fun and education through a unique experience that includes rides and attractions, indoor skydiving, sports activities, creative workshops, science experiments, team challenges, and much more.

Every week brings a new world to explore, ensuring a fresh and exciting experience throughout the summer.

FOUR EXCITING WORLDS

Week 1: Prehistoric World

Step back in time and discover the fascinating world of dinosaurs through themed activities, workshops, games, and adventures.

Week 2: Space Explorers

Embark on a journey across the galaxy with exciting space-themed challenges, experiments, and discoveries.

Week 3: Under the Sea

Dive into the wonders of ocean life through creative activities, interactive experiences, and aquatic adventures.

Week 4: Around the World

Explore different countries and cultures through engaging activities, games, crafts, and global adventures.
