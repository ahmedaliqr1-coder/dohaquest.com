Help & FAQs

Quick answers to help you get what you need faster.

How many rides are there in Doha Quest?

Doha Quest features over 30 rides and attractions, catering to toddlers, families, and thrill-seekers of all ages. To know more, check out the full list of our rides and attractions!

How long do people usually stay?

Most visitors spend 3–4 hours exploring our indoor theme park in Doha to enjoy a mix of thrill rides, interactive zones, and Time Realms. There’s no rush – you’re free to explore at your own pace and make the day your own adventure!

What are the age/height requirements?

Each ride has its own safety criteria. For example, thrill rides such as EpiQ Coaster and Magma Blast require a minimum height of 140 cm for unaccompanied guests, and 130 cm for persons accompanied by an adult. Family rides such as the enchanting carousel are designed for riders aged 3+, with adult-accompanied options available. Exact requirements are posted on our Rides page.

Do you offer birthday packages?

Yes! We offer tailored birthday packages that include rides, themed decorations, cakes and special meals, and other special perks to make your child’s day unforgettable. To book, visit our Celebrations page.

What is your refund policy?

All ticket purchases are non-refundable and non-transferable, except in the case of an event cancellation by Doha Quest.

If you experience an issue with your booking or visit, please contact our Guest Services team as soon as possible — we’ll do our best to help.

Where is Doha Quest located?

Doha Quest is located inside Doha Oasis, on Al Khaleej Street in Msheireb, Downtown Doha. We’re within walking distance of Msheireb Metro Station, and just minutes away from major hotels, Souq Waqif, and the Corniche. See map and directions to Doha Quest here.

Is Doha Quest an Indoor theme park?

Yes! Doha Quest is Qatar’s first and largest indoor theme park, fully climate-controlled for year-round comfort.

What is Doha Quest known for?

Doha Quest boasts the world’s tallest indoor roller coaster and drop tower, along with immersive rides and a combination of thrill, edutainment, and family fun. And let’s not forget iFLY Quest — the ultimate indoor skydiving experience.

Is there transportation available from major hotels or malls in Doha?

We don’t offer direct shuttle services, but Doha Quest is conveniently accessible via taxi, rideshare, and especially the Doha Metro. Hop off at Msheireb Station — just a short walk away — and you’ll find Doha Quest in Doha Oasis

Are there attractions accessible to children and families?

Yes! The park includes a wide variety of attractions for kids, families, and adults. Some rides have height and safety restrictions, which are clearly displayed.

What types of food and dining options are available inside the park?

Doha Quest offers casual dining and snack options, including Quest Cafe, Sarab Kiosk and Space Cantina. Planet Hollywood Doha is also easily accessible through Quest grounds.

Can I purchase official Doha Quest merchandise at the park?

Definitely! Our Time Quest retail store offers exclusive Doha Quest merchandise, apparel, souvenirs, and gifts. You can also shop cool Quest gear online.

What are the ticket types and admission policies?

We offer the following General Admission tickets:

Adults (13 years and up): QAR 235)
Juniors (4 to 12 years): QAR 160
Children under 4: Free to enter

Want to skip queues on specific rides? For an additional QAR 100, you can purchase a Time Portal fast-track ticket.

Is there parking available at Doha Oasis for Doha Quest visitors?

Yes, ample underground parking is available at Doha Oasis, with direct access to Doha Quest via elevators and escalators.

Don’t forget to get your parking ticket stamped at the Quest Info Desk to enjoy free parking.

How can I apply for a job or learn about careers at Doha Quest?

We’re always on the lookout for passionate and talented individuals to join our team.

To explore career opportunities, please visit the Careers section on our website or send your CV to careers@dohaquest.com.
